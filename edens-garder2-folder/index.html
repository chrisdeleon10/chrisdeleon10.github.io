About

Eden's Garden is a local landscaping company servicing the Wake County area in Raleigh, North Carolina. Founded in 2014 owner, Jesus Mora, brings more than 6 years of experience to the table and oversees all of his projects personally. From mowing, weed whacking, and edging your lawn to mulch and rock additions, Eden's Garden is sure to provide a clean and crisp look to give your home the maximum curb appeal.
